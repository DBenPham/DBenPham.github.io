Dodge Ball

Game

Dodge Ball is a survival game where you stay in a circle and try to
dodge ball. The ball doesn't spawn in one place but randomly in one of
the corners.Every 10 seconds, the player obtains 100 points. After 7
seconds, another ball will spawn. The game ends when the square is hit
by the ball.

I chose to make the game because is simple to make but hard to play.
Two successful things about the game are that the balls spawn one
one at a time and stay bouncing until the game ends and the sprite
increases in size if out of the circle.Two obstacles were spawning a
single ball at a time and getting the ball to increase in size when
when out of the circle.I overcome these problems by setting a timer
after creating a clone and putting if a certain color increase.If I
had more time, I would of changed the difficulties since it is buggy
the harder it is.

This code repeats until the player is touch and if it is not, the game
will randomly choose a number from 1 to 4 and spawn more balls after a few
seconds in the specific random corner.
